Frequently Asked Questions

We offer a convenient payment plan designed to make your education more accessible. To begin, you pay 10% of the total course cost upfront. The remaining balance is divided into equal monthly payments based on the duration of your course. For the Nail Technician and Esthetician programs, the payments are spread over three months. The Hairstylist program is divided over seven months, while the Cosmetician and Barber programs are spread across nine months. This flexible approach ensures you can focus on your studies while managing payments with ease.

Refunds are available based on when a student withdraws or is terminated. If withdrawal happens within the first 10% of the program, 90% of tuition is refunded if paid in full, decreasing to 75%, 50%, or 25% as more of the program is completed. No refunds are provided after 75% of the program is completed. Refunds are processed within 30 days of the official termination date, and a full refund is issued if the school discontinues the program and it cannot be completed.

Your training will be personally supervised and led by Laura Sanchez and experienced instructors, ensuring a high-quality and professional learning experience.

Our free consultation is an opportunity to discuss your career goals, explore our programs, and find the best fit for you. We'll answer any questions you have and guide you through the enrollment process.

Enrollment is simple! After your free consultation, we'll assist you with the application, discuss payment options, and schedule your classes to get you started on your new career path.
